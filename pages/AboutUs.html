Our Company - Game Universe Entertainment

Welcome to Game Universe Entertainment, where we create exciting and innovative games to satisfy the appetites of the most demanding gamers. We are a team of talanted developers, designers and creative craftsmen, tired of the ordinary and eager to make our mark on the world of gaming.
